Understanding E-Challans

What is an E-Challan?

An e-challan is an electronic traffic ticket issued for violating traffic rules. It's generated automatically through surveillance cameras or manually by traffic police.

Common Traffic Violations

  • Red light violation
  • Speeding
  • No parking violations
  • Driving without helmet
  • Using mobile while driving

Fine Payment Process

  1. Check challan status
  2. View violation details
  3. Pay fine online
  4. Download receipt
